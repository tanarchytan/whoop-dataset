# Your contribution folder

1. **Copy this whole `_TEMPLATE` folder** to `contributions/<device>/<your-name>/`, where:
   - `<device>` is your WHOOP model: `3.0`, `4.0`, `5.0`, or `mg`
   - `<your-name>` is a **username** (e.g. `alex`) **or** an anonymous id (e.g. `user-0001`) - your choice

   Example: `contributions/5.0/user-0001/`
2. Put your exported CSVs in it:
   - `physiological_cycles.csv`
   - `sleeps.csv`
   - `workouts.csv`
3. **Do NOT add `journal_entries.csv`** or any screenshots / PDFs / profile files.
4. Fill in `metadata.yml` (set `device` to match the folder you put this in).
5. Delete this `PLACE_YOUR_CSVS_HERE.md` file from your copy.

If your data spans more than one WHOOP model, make a folder under each device with that device's data.

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../../CONTRIBUTING.md) for the full guide.
